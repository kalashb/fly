About FLY

Making education interesting for all.

Fun Learning Youth (FLY) is a youth-led organization utilizing cohort-based mentorship to reduce dropout within middle-school underprivileged students in India.

We are not trying to "innovate" for its own sake. We are trying to be present: consistent, kind, and clear about why school matters when life gets heavy.

Our story

Pooja is a good friend of our co-founders. At the age of 14, during the pandemic, she paused going to school and did not have devices to attend online classes. Soon her family decided they would let her work as a house help and let go of education. They planned to marry her off as she would turn 18.

But how will she ever progress, be more independent, and follow her passion? Does she even know what her passion is? Talking to Pooja about this was the turning point. She wanted to study. The situation did not let her.

Our co-founders started teaching Pooja and her brother in their backyard. One day, Pooja asked if she could bring friends along. Why not?

Soon they were teaching 20 students in the same backyard. If one street could have so many students in need of interesting education, how many could there be in the city, country, and world? And FLY was born.

Supporters and recognition

We are incorporated as a nonprofit with Corporations Canada. Local and national newspapers have helped us spread the word about retention rates in schools. Generous supporters have funded our work, and we are grateful for people who give time, advice, and resources - especially in crucial moments.
