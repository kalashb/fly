Computer-based centres and Shikshak

We supported the setup of two computer-based centres with collaborators, then introduced our AI tutor Shikshak so students can explore guided practice.

What this enables

Access + consistency + guided digital learning

Students get structured time on computers, practical digital exposure, and support from mentors on the ground. This complements in-person learning circles and helps build confidence.
